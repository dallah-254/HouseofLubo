Our Story

Founded in 2010 by siblings Lucia and Bosco Okoth, House of LUBO began as a small boutique in Nairobi with a simple vision: to create exquisite jewelry that celebrates African heritage while embracing global sophistication.

What started as a passion project has grown into one of East Africa's most respected luxury jewelry brands, known for our commitment to quality, ethical sourcing, and exceptional craftsmanship.

Ethical Sourcing

We are committed to responsible sourcing of all our materials, ensuring that every piece tells a story of beauty without compromise.

Artisan Craftsmanship

Each piece is meticulously handcrafted by our skilled artisans, blending traditional techniques with contemporary design.

Customer Passion

We believe jewelry should be as unique as the person wearing it, which is why we offer personalized service and custom designs.

Our Values

At House of LUBO, our values are the foundation of everything we do. They guide our decisions, shape our culture, and define our relationships with customers, partners, and our community.

Quality Excellence

We never compromise on quality. Every piece undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure it meets our exacting standards.

Sustainability

We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact through responsible sourcing and eco-friendly practices.

Integrity

We believe in transparency and honesty in all our business dealings, from sourcing to sales.

Community

We support local artisans and contribute to community development initiatives across East Africa.

Our Journey

From our humble beginnings to becoming a leading luxury jewelry brand in East Africa

2010

House of LUBO opens its first boutique in Nairobi's city center, focusing on custom designs for local clients.

2014

We expand to our second location at The Village Market and launch our first international collection.

2018

House of LUBO goes global with our e-commerce platform, shipping to customers across Africa and beyond.

2023

We celebrate 13 years of excellence with over 10,000 pieces sold and recognition as a leading luxury brand.

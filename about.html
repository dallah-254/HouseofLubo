Where Heritage Meets Modern Elegance

Founded in 2010 by Lubaina and Bolaji Okonjo, House of LUBO began as a small Nairobi boutique with a vision to redefine African luxury jewelry. Our name, LUBO, combines the founders' names, symbolizing the perfect union of tradition and innovation.

What started as a passion project has blossomed into East Africa's premier destination for exquisite jewelry, celebrated for its unique blend of contemporary design and traditional African artistry.

Each piece in our collection tells a story - of skilled artisans preserving centuries-old techniques, of rare gemstones sourced ethically from across the continent, and of modern design sensibilities that make every creation both timeless and contemporary.

Our Values

The principles that guide every piece we create and every relationship we build

Excellence in Craftsmanship

Every piece is meticulously handcrafted by our master artisans, combining traditional techniques with innovative design to create heirloom-quality jewelry.

Ethical Sourcing

We partner directly with mining communities across Africa to ensure fair trade practices and sustainable sourcing of all our gemstones and materials.

Community Impact

Through our LUBO Foundation, we support artisan training programs and education initiatives in communities where our materials are sourced.

Heritage Preservation

We work to preserve traditional African jewelry-making techniques while adapting them for contemporary luxury markets.

Personal Connection

We believe jewelry should tell your story. Our bespoke service ensures each piece reflects the unique personality and journey of its wearer.

African Pride

We celebrate the rich diversity of African cultures through designs that honor traditional symbolism while embracing global luxury aesthetics.

Our Journey

Milestones in our story of growth, innovation, and excellence

2010

Foundation

House of LUBO opens its first boutique in Nairobi's Village Market, introducing a new vision for African luxury jewelry.

2013

Artisan Partnership

We establish partnerships with master artisans across Kenya, Tanzania, and Ethiopia, preserving traditional techniques.

2015

International Recognition

Our Tsavorite collection wins the International Jewelry Design Excellence Award, marking our global debut.

2018

Sustainability Initiative

Launch of our ethical sourcing program, ensuring fair trade and environmental responsibility throughout our supply chain.

2021

Digital Expansion

We launch our e-commerce platform, bringing House of LUBO to luxury clients worldwide while maintaining our boutique experience.

2023

LUBO Foundation

Establishment of the LUBO Foundation to support artisan education and preserve African jewelry heritage for future generations.

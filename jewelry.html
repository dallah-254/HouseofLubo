Jewelry Care Guide

Learn how to maintain the beauty and longevity of your precious jewelry

Cleaning

Regularly clean your jewelry with a soft cloth and mild soap solution to maintain its sparkle.

Storage

Store jewelry in a dry, cool place away from direct sunlight and in separate compartments.

Protection

Remove jewelry before swimming, showering, or applying cosmetics and perfumes.

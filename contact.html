Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services

Do you offer custom jewelry design services?

Yes, we specialize in custom jewelry design. Our master artisans work closely with you to create unique pieces that reflect your personal style and story. The process begins with a consultation where we discuss your vision, preferences, and budget.

Custom orders typically take 4-6 weeks to complete, depending on complexity. We provide regular updates throughout the creation process.

What is your jewelry cleaning and maintenance policy?

We offer complimentary cleaning and inspection services for all House of LUBO jewelry. We recommend bringing your pieces in for professional cleaning every 6 months to maintain their brilliance.

Additionally, we provide a lifetime warranty on craftsmanship for all our pieces. For detailed care instructions specific to your jewelry type, please refer to our care guide or contact our customer service team.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International orders typically take 7-14 business days for delivery, depending on the destination. Shipping costs and import duties vary by country and will be calculated at checkout.

All international shipments are fully insured and require signature upon delivery. We recommend checking your country's customs regulations regarding jewelry imports before placing an order.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact. Custom pieces and engraved items cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect.

Exchanges can be made within 60 days of purchase. For returns or exchanges, please contact our customer service team to initiate the process. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

How can I book a private consultation?

Private consultations can be booked through our website, by phone, or by visiting any of our boutiques. During a private consultation, you'll receive personalized attention from our jewelry experts in a comfortable, private setting.

We offer consultations for various needs including engagement ring selection, custom design, jewelry styling, and collection viewing. Appointments typically last 60-90 minutes and can be scheduled during or after regular business hours.

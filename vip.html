Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our VIP program

How do I become a VIP member?

You can become a VIP member by selecting your preferred membership tier and completing the enrollment process. Once enrolled, you'll receive your VIP welcome kit and immediate access to all member benefits.

Can I upgrade my membership tier?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership tier at any time. The difference in annual fee will be prorated based on the time remaining in your current membership period.

Is there a family or couple membership option?

Currently, we offer individual memberships only. However, you can purchase multiple memberships under separate accounts if you wish to extend VIP benefits to family members.

How do I access the VIP-only collection?

Once you become a VIP member, you'll receive a unique login to our VIP portal where you can browse and purchase from our exclusive collection. Our VIP concierge can also assist you with selections.

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your VIP membership at any time. However, membership fees are non-refundable, and benefits will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle.
